**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The French Bulldog! 🐶**

**Description:**
The French Bulldog is a charming, compact, and irresistibly lovable breed that has taken the world by storm. With their signature bat ears, wrinkled faces, and fun-loving personalities, Frenchies are the ultimate urban companion — equally happy snuggling on the couch or exploring the neighbourhood with their favourite person.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Easygoing, happy, and playful. French Bulldogs are fun-loving and outgoing, getting along well with children, other pets, and visitors alike.
-        **Size:** Small and muscular, weighing under 13 kg and standing 28–33 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Short, smooth coat that is easy to maintain. Average shedders requiring only occasional brushing.
-        **Exercise Needs:** Moderate — daily short walks and indoor play sessions suit them well. They are not suited for intense exercise due to their brachycephalic (flat-faced) structure.

**Why Choose a French Bulldog?**
-        **Perfect City Dog:** Their compact size and low exercise requirements make them ideal for apartment living and urban lifestyles.
-        **Laid-Back Companion:** French Bulldogs are naturally calm and adaptable, thriving in homes where they receive plenty of affection.
-        **Big Personality, Small Package:** Despite their size, Frenchies are full of character and bring enormous joy and laughter to their families.
