**Description:**
The Golden Retriever is one of the most beloved dog breeds in the world, cherished for its sweet-natured temperament and unwavering loyalty. With a lustrous golden coat and a heart full of warmth, this intelligent and gentle breed is the perfect addition to any family, thriving equally as a playful companion and a devoted service dog.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Friendly, gentle, and highly intelligent. Golden Retrievers are known for their patience with children, warmth toward strangers, and deep loyalty to their families.
-        **Size:** Medium to large, typically weighing between 25-34 kg.
-        **Coat:** Thick, water-repellent double coat with a dense undercoat. Sheds considerably year-round; regular brushing keeps it healthy and gleaming.
-        **Exercise Needs:** Active and energetic, they thrive on daily walks, fetch sessions, and outdoor adventures. They also love swimming.

**Why Choose a Golden Retriever?**
-        **Family Favourite:** Their gentle nature makes them outstanding with children and other pets, earning their reputation as the quintessential family dog.
-        **Highly Trainable:** Golden Retrievers consistently rank among the easiest breeds to train, excelling in obedience, agility, and service roles.
-        **Lifelong Loyalty:** Known for forming deep bonds with their owners, they offer unconditional love and companionship every single day.
