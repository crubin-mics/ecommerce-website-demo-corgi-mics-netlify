**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Pomeranian! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Pomeranian is a tiny, fluffy ball of energy and personality that has charmed dog lovers for centuries. Named after the Pomerania region of Central Europe, these spirited little dogs carry themselves with remarkable confidence and possess a luxurious double coat that makes them instantly recognisable. Playful, intelligent, and endlessly affectionate, Pomeranians are perfect companions for those who want a small dog with a big heart.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Sassy, spirited, and intelligent. Pomeranians are confident, curious, and enjoy learning new things. They get along well with other pets and thrive on attention from their owners.
-        **Size:** Tiny — weighing only 1.5-3 kg and standing 18–20 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Dense, fluffy double coat in a wide range of colours including orange, white, black, cream, sable, and brindle. Requires consistent grooming and daily brushing during shedding seasons.
-        **Exercise Needs:** Low to moderate — short daily walks and indoor play sessions are sufficient to keep them happy and healthy.

**Why Choose a Pomeranian?**
-        **Portable Companion:** Their tiny size makes them ideal travel companions and perfect for apartment or small-space living.
-        **Big Personality:** Pomeranians are alert, lively, and curious — never boring, always entertaining.
-        **Variety of Colours:** One of the most colour-diverse breeds, allowing you to find the perfect match for your taste.
