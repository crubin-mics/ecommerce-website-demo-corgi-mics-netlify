**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Beagle! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Beagle is a cheerful, curious, and loving breed that has captured hearts around the world for centuries. Originally bred as a scent hound for hunting, today's Beagle brings those same traits of determination and energy into family life — making them endlessly entertaining and deeply affectionate companions for households of all sizes.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Loving, affectionate, and social. Beagles are generally excellent with children and other animals, and their curious nature keeps them perpetually engaged with the world around them.
-        **Size:** Compact and sturdy, typically weighing under 14 kg and standing 30–38 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Short, dense double coat that sheds moderately year-round. Low-maintenance grooming with weekly brushing recommended.
-        **Exercise Needs:** Energetic and food-motivated, they need daily exercise and mental stimulation to prevent boredom. A securely fenced yard is ideal.

**Why Choose a Beagle?**
-        **Compact and Adaptable:** Their manageable size and adaptable nature make them well-suited to both apartments and larger homes, as long as they get sufficient exercise.
-        **Sociable Personality:** Beagles thrive in the company of people and other dogs, making them a great choice for multi-pet households.
-        **Expressive and Entertaining:** Known for their vocal nature and expressive faces, Beagles bring personality and laughter to any home.
