**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Yorkshire Terrier! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Yorkshire Terrier, affectionately known as the Yorkie, is a tiny dog with a massive personality. Despite their delicate appearance, Yorkies are bold, brave, and bursting with energy. Their long, silky coat and spirited character have made them one of the most popular toy breeds in the world — a glamorous yet feisty companion that fits perfectly in any home.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Brave, affectionate, and bold. Yorkies are confident beyond their size and typically affectionate with both family and strangers, though each dog has its own personality.
-        **Size:** Tiny, weighing up to 3 kg and standing 18–23 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Long, straight, silky coat that closely resembles human hair. Requires significant grooming — daily brushing for long coats, weekly for trimmed styles.
-        **Exercise Needs:** Moderate — daily short walks and indoor play sessions meet their exercise needs. They adapt well to apartment living.

**Why Choose a Yorkshire Terrier?**
-        **Compact and Travel-Friendly:** Their small size makes them perfect companions for people on the go, whether in a city apartment or a busy family home.
-        **Big Character:** Yorkies pack enormous personality into a tiny frame, providing endless entertainment and affection for their owners.
-        **Hypoallergenic-Friendly Coat:** Their hair-like coat sheds minimally, making them a popular choice for allergy-sensitive households.
