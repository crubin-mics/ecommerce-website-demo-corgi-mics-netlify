**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Rottweiler! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Rottweiler is a powerful, loyal, and confident breed with a heart of gold beneath its imposing exterior. Originally bred as a working and guardian dog, today's Rottweiler is equally known for being a devoted family companion. When properly trained and socialised, Rottweilers are calm, gentle, and deeply affectionate with their loved ones.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Loyal, confident, and calm. Rottweilers are naturally protective of their families and can range from affectionate with everyone to dedicated one-person dogs depending on their personality.
-        **Size:** Large and muscular, typically weighing between 36-61 kg and standing 56–69 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Short, dense, smooth coat that sheds heavily year-round. Weekly brushing helps manage shedding.
-        **Exercise Needs:** High — Rottweilers need daily vigorous exercise including long walks, runs, and mentally stimulating activities to stay healthy and well-balanced.

**Why Choose a Rottweiler?**
-        **Natural Protector:** Their instinct to guard and protect makes them outstanding watchdogs and loyal guardians for families and properties.
-        **Intelligent and Trainable:** Rottweilers are highly intelligent and excel in obedience, protection sports, and working roles when given consistent, positive training.
-        **Devoted Companion:** Behind the strength and confidence is a dog that craves love and closeness with its family — a truly loyal and rewarding companion.
