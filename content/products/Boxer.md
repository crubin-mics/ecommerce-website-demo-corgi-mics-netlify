**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Boxer! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Boxer is an exuberant, playful, and fiercely loyal breed that brings boundless energy and devotion to every family it joins. Muscular and athletic on the outside, yet gentle and affectionate at heart, Boxers are known for their love of play, their patience with children, and their instinct to protect the ones they love.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Happy, energetic, and loyal. Boxers are intelligent and trainable, with a playful spirit that endures well into adulthood — they are often described as "forever puppies."
-        **Size:** Medium to large, typically weighing between 29-36 kg and standing 53–63 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Short, smooth, and tight-fitting coat. Minimal grooming required; moderate shedding managed with weekly brushing.
-        **Exercise Needs:** High — Boxers need plenty of daily exercise including brisk walks, runs, and vigorous play to channel their energy positively.

**Why Choose a Boxer?**
-        **Wonderful with Children:** Their playful, patient nature makes them outstanding family dogs, especially in homes with active kids.
-        **Natural Guardian:** Boxers are instinctively protective and alert, making them dependable watchdogs without being aggressive.
-        **Joyful Companion:** Their enthusiasm for life is infectious — a Boxer will bring laughter, energy, and warmth to any household every single day.
