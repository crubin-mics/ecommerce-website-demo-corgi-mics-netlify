**🐾 Meet Your New Loyal Companion: The Samoyed! 🐶**

**Description:**
The Samoyed is a breathtaking breed, instantly recognisable by its cloud-like white coat and perpetual "Sammy smile." Originating from Siberia where they worked alongside nomadic tribes as sled dogs and companions, today's Samoyeds bring that same warmth, intelligence, and playfulness into family life. They are as beautiful as they are loving — a truly special breed.

**Key Features:**
-        **Temperament:** Playful, alert, and friendly. Samoyeds are clever and quick-witted, with a mischievous streak. They get along well with children, other dogs, and cats when properly socialised.
-        **Size:** Medium to large, typically weighing between 16-30 kg and standing 48–60 cm tall.
-        **Coat:** Thick two-layer coat — a long straight topcoat and a dense soft undercoat, always white or cream. Requires consistent brushing due to year-round shedding.
-        **Exercise Needs:** High — Samoyeds need daily exercise including long walks, runs, and outdoor play. Their working heritage means they thrive with an active lifestyle.

**Why Choose a Samoyed?**
-        **Stunning Appearance:** Their pristine white coat and iconic smile make Samoyeds one of the most visually striking dog breeds in the world.
-        **Gentle and Social:** Known for their friendliness with both people and animals, Samoyeds are wonderful family dogs and natural people-pleasers.
-        **Devoted Working Spirit:** Intelligent and eager to please, they excel in various dog sports and thrive when given a purpose or regular mental challenges.
